# Reflection

## High-Energy Pop vs Chill Lofi
The High-Energy Pop profile surfaced upbeat, fast songs like Sunrise City and Gym Hero. The Chill Lofi profile shifted completely to low-energy, slow songs like Library Rain and Midnight Coding. This makes sense because both genre and energy pulled in opposite directions for these two profiles.

## Chill Lofi vs Deep Intense Rock
The Chill Lofi profile favored quiet, acoustic songs while Deep Intense Rock favored Storm Runner exclusively due to its genre and mood match. The energy scoring reinforced this — rock songs cluster near 0.9 energy while lofi songs cluster near 0.35, so the gap between profiles is large.

## What I learned
Even a simple three-rule scoring system produces results that feel intuitive. The genre weight of +2.0 dominates the rankings, which shows how much a single design choice shapes what users see. Real platforms face the same problem at a much larger scale.
